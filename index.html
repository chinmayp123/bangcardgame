BANG!
The Wild West Card Game
Based on the card game by Emiliano Sciarra
How to Play

The Story

The Outlaws hunt the Sheriff. The Sheriff hunts the Outlaws. The Deputy protects the Sheriff. The Renegade wants to be the last one standing.

Roles

⭐Sheriff — revealed at start. Wins when all Outlaws and Renegade are dead.
🛡️Deputy — hidden. Wins with the Sheriff.
🔴Outlaw — hidden. Wins when the Sheriff is dead.
💀Renegade — hidden. Must be the last player alive.

Your Turn

1. Draw — Draw 2 cards from the deck.

2. Play — Play any number of cards (but only 1 BANG! per turn unless your character says otherwise).

3. Discard — End your turn. If you have more cards than your HP, you must discard down.

Key Cards

BANG! — Shoot a player in range. They must play a MISS! or lose 1 life.

MISS! — Cancel a BANG! aimed at you.

Beer — Recover 1 life point. Doesn't work with only 2 players left.

Panic! — Steal a card from a player at distance 1.

Cat Balou — Force any player to discard a card.

Distance & Weapons

Players sit in a circle. Your default range is 1 (only neighbors). Weapons increase your range. A Scope lets you see 1 farther. A Mustang makes others see you 1 farther away.

Blue & Green Cards

Blue cards stay in play in front of you (weapons, Barrel, Jail, etc.).

Green cards (Dodge City) are played face-up but can't be used until your next turn.

Tips

Watch who attacks whom — it reveals alliances. Protect the Sheriff if you're a Deputy. As an Outlaw, gang up on the Sheriff. As the Renegade, keep the balance until the end.
